17 Days Old
Here is a picture of Warren at 17 days old. Warren’s eyes have now opened and he can see the world for the first time. His eyesight won’t be perfect just yet but he’ll be able to make out different shapes of objects. Gerbils are curious creatures by nature and young Warren is no exception. He spends all day poking around inquisitively, sniffing and nibbling the various things that are in his tank. A Yahoo! Camera crew are on site to document the daily activities of the gerbil for the next 12 months! You’ll be able to see the very latest footage in and around tonight’s Channel 5 movie.

Why are we following the life of a Gerbil?

These days almost every programme is sponsored by some company or other. All those jingles and logos can be a bit tedious, not to mention annoying. So, when Yahoo! secured the sponsorship of the Channel 5 Movie, we decided to do something different. The lifecycle of a gerbil is around a year, the same length of time as our sponsorship. We could think of no cooler way of filling the airtime than to follow this fascinating creature, each day, during the Channel 5 Movie, for the next 12 months.
